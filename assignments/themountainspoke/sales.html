The Mountain Spoke Bike Shop

We have many different shapes and styles of bicycles. We know how hard it is to compare some of the top brands when you have to click back and forth between the bicycle company sites. We have put four of the top Brands we sell, side by side so you not only see the bikes but you are also able to read about it and check the price.

    • The Italian brand, Pinarello, is best known for its quality road bikes, although it also produces cyclocross, track, mountain and city bikes. They're also British Cycling's bike of choice, according to Bike Radar. Price ranging from $2,500 to $5,125.
    • Raleigh has an iconic place in cycling history. It made high quality commuter bikes that fit a modest budget. The frame is made of steel. Bikes range in price from $275 to $690 for comfort bikes, $520 to $1,550 for fitness bikes, $275 to $1,325 for mountain bikes, and $520 to $2,725 for road bikes, according to Consumer Reports.
    • Giant gets high ratings in both the mountain and hybrid bikes categories because of the bikes’ design and features. Cyclists who want a comfortable and confident ride, both on and off the road, consider Giant first. Prices range from $607.99 to $11,905. “And it’s also a good entry-level bike.”
    • Felt bikes feel as if their brand puts the same effort into every bike, whether it’s a bike for cruising around town, crushing local single track, or setting your personal best at a triathlon. Felt develops every part of every bike down to the littlest details with in-house engineers. Prices ranging from $1,785 to $8,999.
